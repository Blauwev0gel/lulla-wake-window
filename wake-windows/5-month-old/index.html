5-month-old wake windows

Five months often sits between a four-nap and three-nap rhythm. Some days still need a short late nap.

Part of dayStarting range
First wake window1h30–2h
Later wake windows1h40–2h30
Likely rhythm3–4 naps

Three naps or four?

A three-nap day may fit when earlier naps are longer. A fourth nap may still be useful after short naps or an early morning start. The number of naps is an outcome of the day, not a target that must be forced.

Suggested nap length

Main naps may fall roughly in the 45–90 minute range, while a late bridging nap is often shorter. Natural variation is normal.

Signs the day needs another nap

  • Bedtime would otherwise require an unusually long final wake window.
  • Earlier naps were short.
  • Your baby is clearly struggling well before bedtime.
