About Lulla

Lulla exists to answer one practical question quickly: when is the baby likely to sleep next?

The tool is browser-based, free to use and does not require an account. Its focused design avoids becoming a feeding, nappy or development tracker.

What Lulla does

It estimates the next nap or bedtime, displays the wake-window length and suggests a flexible nap duration.

What Lulla does not do

It does not provide medical advice, diagnose sleep issues or promise a perfect schedule.

Methodology · Privacy · Terms

General sleep-planning guidance, not medical advice.
