Privacy

Lulla is designed to minimise data collection.

Calculator data

Your selected age, wake time and rhythm preferences are stored locally in your browser so the tool can remember them. The free calculator does not require an account.

Analytics

If privacy-friendly site analytics are enabled, they should measure broad usage and performance without sending exact baby sleep times or names.

Delete local data

You can clear the site’s local data through your browser settings. Clearing browser storage resets Lulla.
